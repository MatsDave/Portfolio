About me

Hello! I’m Meetee Dave, a passionate and aspiring Full Stack Developer with a growing focus in Data Science using Python. Whether it’s debugging code or deriving insights from data, I bring critical thinking and a positive attitude to the table. Let’s connect and build something amazing together!
